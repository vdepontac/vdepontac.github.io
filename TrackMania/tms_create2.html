NADEO presents Mania\Create II : Summer Edition

Builders Assemble! Gather a team of 5 players and enter the first TrackMania Sunrise creation contest. Your goal is to create the best TrackMania Sunrise solo campaign using all possible means! Make the first official community campaigns and enter the Mania\Create hall of fame.

Rules:
- The format of the campaign is fixed to 5 series of 3 challenges.
- The choice of environments and challenge types is free.
- The campaign must be accessible to all**.
- Each race challenge will be delivered with a 4 ghosts type replay.
- The campaign challenges, replays and all the additional data files should not weight more than 15Mb.
- All the additional campaign data (custom panels...) must be totally free of any copyright and acceptable for all viewing audiences.
- The entry deadline is august 22, 2005. The campaigns will be ranked by the Nadeo team.

The results will be announced on the 26th of august, 2005.


**(accessible to all)
- if use of text, use simple english
- progressive difficulty
- reasonable hardware requirements: 2000 coppers average per track, limited use of post process fx during a race
- author times made with keyboard
- advice: ask other players (beginner and expert) to test drive your campaign

Additional rules:
- a single entry for each team, and a single team for each player (multiplayer id)
- You may use data from people who are not a part of your team, if those people allowed you to do so. The original track authors may leave their name on their tracks. Originality, exclusivity and the overall theme of the campaign are important factors.

NADEO team - maniacreate@nadeo.com
