3D Models & Skinning contest : CarMania!
CARPARK and SKINCENTER team is happy to announce the launch of their own 3d modelisation contest : CarMania!

The aim is quite simple, you only have to propose the best vehicle ever for TMS! This will be done by teams of 2 : 1 Modeler/Modder and 1 skinner, this to show up skills of both SKINCENTER skinners and CARPARK modelers!

Numerous prizes are to be won, offered by Focus and TM-Shop!

Deadline on November 16th!
