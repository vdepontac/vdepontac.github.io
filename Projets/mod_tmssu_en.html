TrackMania Sunrise Speed-Up!

Mod release date: September 30, 2021


TrackMania Sunrise: Speed-Up! is a beta content mod for TMS.

Participation in development alongside the TrackMania Modding Community

Development of the mod for TmSunrise began in February-March of 2021. During this period, a lot of content appeared and was modified in the mod. Initially, development was launched in late 2018-early 2019. At that time, the mod was based on the game TM United 2006 (not Forever), but after the Win10 patch was released by KRBDZSKL, the mod immediately switched to TMS.

In this mod, content from the beta version will be restored and recreated: tracks, skins, interface, sounds, etc.
